Privacy Policy

CCPA & COPPA Compliant

Last Updated: May 22, 2025

Your Privacy: OnkLabs develops mobile games that are completely offline and do not collect, store, or share any personal information. This policy explains how we handle your data and your rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and other applicable regulations.

1. About OnkLabs

OnkLabs develops mobile games for personal use, designed to be fully offline. We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially for California residents and children under 13.

  • Developer: OnkLabs (Individual)
  • Contact: onklabs@gmail.com
  • Location: Vietnam
  • Website: onklabs.github.io

2. Personal Information We Collect

No Personal Information Collected: Our apps are completely offline and do not collect, store, or share any personal information, such as identifiers, internet activity, or commercial information. All game data, such as progress or settings, is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not collect or use any personal information. Game data stored locally on your device is used solely to provide app functionality, such as saving game progress.

  • App Functionality: Provide gaming features and save progress locally on your device.
  • No Advertising: Our apps do not display advertisements or use advertising services.
  • Customer Service: Respond to support requests using only the information you provide voluntarily.

4. Information Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties, as no personal information is collected by our apps.

5. Children's Privacy

  • Under 13: Our app for children under 13 does not collect any personal information, so parental consent is not required.
  • 13-15 years: As our app does not collect or share personal information, no opt-in consent is needed for any "sale" under CCPA.
  • Parents: Since no personal information is collected, there is no data for parents to exercise CCPA rights over. All app data is stored locally on the child's device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

6. Your California Rights

  • Right to Know: We do not collect any personal information, so there is no personal data to disclose.
  • Right to Delete: As all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it by uninstalling the app.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Our app does not collect, share, or sell any personal information, so the concept of "sale" under CCPA does not apply, and no opt-out is required.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: You will not face different treatment, as we do not collect or process personal data.
  • Right to Correct: Since no personal information is collected, there is no data to correct.

7. How to Exercise Your Rights

  • Contact: Since our app does not collect personal information, there is no personal data to exercise CCPA rights over. All application data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
  • Questions: If you have any inquiries about your privacy rights, contact us at onklabs@gmail.com with the subject "Privacy Inquiry".
  • Response Time: We will respond to your inquiries within 45 days, as required by CCPA.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app is completely offline and does not use any third-party services, such as advertising platforms, to collect or process personal information.

9. Contact Us

  • Email: onklabs@gmail.com

  • Response: We will respond within 45 days, as required by CCPA.
